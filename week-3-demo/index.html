Bearing the weight of legal compliance and contractual restrictions is a difficult task.

At best, licensing music from mainstream sources is a costly and time consuming process, and at the end of the line there is little in any one library to differentiate you from your competitors. Or YouTube.

BAMM.tv helps partners move the “music” category from a cost driver to a profit center with innovative strategies that deliver localized, customized, exclusive content for your customers. Music on every screen

In a digital world, users expect to be able to access their content on any device. BAMM.tv makes the multi-screen experience a plug-and-play feature for your business.

A single content license extends global rights to all your territories and guarantees that customers will be able to access content from web, mobile, tablet and television at no additional cost.

BAMM.tv’s powerful backend and API allows for seamless integration into your existing properties, or we can develop custom mobile, tablet and IPTV apps for your business.

Whatever your needs, BAMM.tv provides the infrastructure and technology for mass distribution across wide networks of carriers and platforms.
